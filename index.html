How to delete your account

  • Open the app homescreen → click on the top right delete account button → Tap Delete Account
  • Confirm by typing "are you sure"

Data Deletion Policy

- All user data will be permanently deleted within 7 days - This includes account information, attendance records, and associated data - Some legal logs may be retained if required
